# Product Sense — NASCAR Analytics

## Vision

Give NASCAR's 75 million fans the clean, modern data tools the sport deserves. The current landscape (Lap Raptor, FRCS.pro, Racing-Reference) has valuable data but terrible UX — stuck in 2003-2008 era design with no mobile experience. We build the platform that makes NASCAR data accessible, beautiful, and actionable.

## Target Users

### Primary: NASCAR Fans Who Love Data
- Watch races weekly, follow standings and stats
- Want deeper insight than the broadcast provides
- Comfortable with stats but not necessarily data scientists
- Mobile-first — check stats on their phone at the track or on the couch

### Secondary: DFS / Fantasy NASCAR Players
- Play DraftKings/FanDuel NASCAR contests (~36 race weekends/year)
- Currently use FRCS.pro, Win The Race, or spreadsheets
- Willing to pay $10-20/month for an edge
- Want lineup optimization, ownership projections, track-type analysis

### Tertiary: NASCAR Bettors
- Bet on race winners, matchup props, futures
- Want data-driven insights on where odds are mispriced
- Currently have no NASCAR-specific betting analytics tool
- High willingness to pay, especially via affiliate conversions

### Future: Short Track Operators (Phase 2)
- Track promoters managing local sponsors
- Need simple CRM for sponsor relationships
- Different product entirely — separate domain

## North Star Metric

**Weekly Active Users who return for 3+ consecutive race weekends.** This measures whether the product is genuinely useful during the NASCAR season, not just a curiosity.

## Product Principles

1. **Data should be beautiful** — If it looks like a spreadsheet from 2003, we've failed
2. **Mobile-first** — Most fans check stats on their phone. Desktop is secondary.
3. **Proprietary metrics are the moat** — Don't just show NASCAR's data. Create unique insights only we have.
4. **Freemium with a clear upgrade path** — Free tier drives SEO and word-of-mouth. Premium unlocks predictions, optimizer, and advanced filters.
5. **Race-weekend cadence** — The product lives and breathes on the NASCAR calendar. Content and features spike around race weekends.

## Competitive Landscape

| Competitor | Strength | Weakness | Our Angle |
|------------|----------|----------|-----------|
| Lap Raptor | Best free analytics (wARP, PFAE) | No monetization, dated UX, no betting | Modern UX + proprietary metrics + betting context |
| FRCS.pro | 21 years of trust, loop data | 2008 UX, no mobile | Mobile-first, cleaner data presentation |
| Win The Race | Monte Carlo sims, DFS optimizer | Paywall-first, no freemium, niche | Freemium funnel, broader audience |
| Racing-Reference | Complete historical database | 2003 UX, no visualizations | Beautiful charts, AI-assisted queries |
| NASCAR Mobile | Official app, scanner audio | Freezes, poor UX, premium paywall | We don't compete on scanner — we compete on analytics |
