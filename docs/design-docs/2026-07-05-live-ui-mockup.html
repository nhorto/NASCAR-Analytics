Live UI — Design Mockup (Phase 3)

The live race companion, in the established house style (dark, caution-yellow accent, condensed display type, tabular numerals). Numbers are illustrative — a live mid-race snapshot of a 2026 Cup road race. Structure is layered: a glanceable board with a race-status header on top, and any car taps open to a full per-driver live panel. Frame 2 is interactive — tap the sort toggle and tap a row. Wordmark "LOOPLAB" is the existing placeholder.
